About Desi Zaiqa

Ghar Ke Masale, Khushiyan Banate Hain

Desi Zaiqa ek aam ghar ki kahani hai, jo har aam ghar ke khane ko khaas banane ka jazba rakhti hai. Hamara maqsad aap tak 100% khalis, behtareen aur saaf suthre masale pohanchana hai. Hum mantay hain ke har lazeez khane ki buniyad uske masalon mein chupi hoti hai.

Hamare masale baghair kisi milawat (no preservatives or artificial colors) ke tayar kiye jate hain, bilkul wese hi jaise dadi-nani ke zamane mein gharon mein peese jate thay. Desi Zaiqa apnain, aur apne khano mein sehat aur lazzat dono shamil karein.
